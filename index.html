SolidWORKS Student Leader Program

SOLIDWORKS is launching a Student Leader Program at a limited number of schools throughout the world. Only certain schools have been chosen to participate at this time, but we’ll be adding more over the next few months

  • Develop leadership qualities that will prepare you for your career
  • Sharpen your skills and become a SOLIDWORKS expert
  • Network with students around the world who are passionate about SOLIDWORKS

What will Student Leaders do? They will communicate important information about SOLIDWORKS to their fellow students, informing them about Student Premium licenses, the SOLIDWORKS Certification Program, internship opportunities, local SOLIDWORKS User Group Network events, and other important topics, activities, and resources.

Certified SolidWORKS Associate

This certification is intended for a student who is well-versed in both basic knowledge of engineering fundamentals and industry practices, along with SOLIDWORKS CAD software and its methodologies and simulation principles.

  • SOLIDWORKS recommends that applicants review the online tutorials on Parts, Assemblies, and Drawings as a prerequisite
  • 150 hours of classroom instruction that thoroughly covers the SOLIDWORKS CAD application
  • Engineering design principles, and in industry best practices.

Certified SolidWORKS Professional

This certification is intended for a student with a minimum of 1 to 2 years of SOLIDWORKS experience and advanced knowledge of engineering practices.

  • SOLIDWORKS recommends that applicants review the online tutorials on Parts, Assemblies, and Drawings as a prerequisite
  • 100 hours of classroom instruction that thoroughly covers the SOLIDWORKS CAD application
  • Engineering design principles, and in industry best practices.

Certified SolidWORKS Expert

A Certified SOLIDWORKS Expert is someone who easily demonstrates the ability to utilize advanced functions and features to solve complex modeling challenges.

  • This certification includes a digital badge issued by Acclaim on behalf of Dassault Systèmes at the successful completion of the test
  • 200 hours of classroom time learning SOLIDWORKS or using SOLIDWORKS with advanced engineering design principles and practices.
  • SOLIDWORKS CAD software and its methodologies and simulation principles.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I apply for the student leader program at Solidworks?

    To apply for the SLP program please reach out to your nearest training center and enroll yourself for free in the program.

  • Do I need to have knowledge of Solidworks at joining

    No, You are not required to have any prior knowledge of Solidworks upon joining the program only knowing computer basics works pretty amazing.

  • What are the benefits of joining the program?

    The benefits upon joining the program are that the student enrolled with getting:

    • International Exposure
    • Expert guidance
    • workshops with Q&A
    • Internship Opportunities.
    • Clarify doubts at any time.
    • vouchers for certifications.
  • What is the duration of the training?

    The duration of training differs on the basis of expertise level. currently,
    we cover:

    • months at the beginner level (Novice)
    • 15 days at the medium level (Necromancer)
    • 1 week for certification at Expert level (Wizard)

    NOTE: There is a mandatory 1-week test series upon qualifying you will be eligible for the certification vouchers worth INR 8000- INR 14000 for FREE.

  • How our skills will be tested during the program?

    During the test series week, you will be provided with the questions asked in the examination. The course has been curated by our experts.

  • Are vouchers free?

    A big YES! Vouchers are absolutely free upon qualifying the test series.
